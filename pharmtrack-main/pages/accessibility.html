Safety & Emergency
Support

Comprehensive tools that guide users through emergencies, prevent medication errors, and ensure every Indian — regardless of age or tech-savviness — can use PharmaTrack with confidence.

Emergency Mode

Finds nearby hospitals using real-time live location data. Displays contact numbers, estimated distance, and the fastest route — reducing panic and critical response time during medical emergencies.

Precautions & Safety Warnings

Provides drug-specific guidance for every medication. Example: Ibuprofen — warns against empty stomach consumption, advises caution for kidney conditions. Covers food interactions and activities to avoid.

Safe Combination Guidance

Warns about unsafe drug-drug interactions in real time. Example: Advises against Iron + Calcium supplements together; recommends timed separation to prevent adverse reactions from polypharmacy.

Medicine Cycle & Reminders

Users set up detailed cycles documenting dosage, frequency, and duration for each medication. Automated reminders prevent missed or repeated doses with fully customizable scheduling. Visual tracking of completed, upcoming, and missed doses across all prescribed medications.

Inclusive Design & Accessibility

Information presented with clear, intuitive icons and minimal text to reduce cognitive load. Designed specifically for the elderly, rural individuals, and non-technical users with large text and high-contrast UI. Universal usability is a core design principle, not an afterthought — every interaction is tested for ease of use.
