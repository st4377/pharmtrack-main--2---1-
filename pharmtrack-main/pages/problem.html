The Challenge

Addressing Critical Gaps
in Healthcare

PharmaTrack tackles real, life-impacting issues that affect millions of patients — especially in India's rural and elderly communities.

💊

Medicine Unavailability

Patients lack knowledge of safe alternatives when prescribed medicines are unavailable, causing dangerous treatment delays and repeated pharmacy visits.

⚠️ High Impact
⚠️

Counterfeit Medicines

Rising prevalence of fake drugs poses serious health risks, leading to ineffective treatments and severe adverse reactions with no way to verify authenticity.

🔴 Critical Risk
🚑

Emergency Preparedness

Panic and time loss occur during emergencies due to lack of immediate access to nearby hospitals, contact numbers, and critical routing information.

⏱️ Time-Sensitive
📋

Dosage Errors

Poor understanding of dosage, precautions, and side effects leads to incorrect usage and serious complications — particularly among elderly patients.

👴 Elderly at Risk
🔗

Unsafe Combinations

Lack of guidance on unsafe drug combinations and missed doses due to absent reminders compounds health risks from polypharmacy in multi-condition patients.

💊 Polypharmacy
🌐

Non-Inclusive Apps

Existing digital solutions are complex, English-centric, and not designed for elderly or rural Indian populations — the people who need help the most.

🌍 Rural Gap

PharmaTrack Solves All of These

One unified platform addressing every critical gap — from verification to emergency support to inclusive design for every Indian user.
